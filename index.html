Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 13, 2025

1. Introduction

VaultX is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use our application.

2. Data Collection

VaultX collects the following types of data:

  • Files (images, videos, documents) that you explicitly choose to store in the app
  • Device information for compatibility and optimization
  • Biometric authentication data (only used locally on your device and never stored by us)

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely for the following purposes:

  • To store and display your files within the secure vault
  • To manage folder organization based on your preferences
  • To apply encryption to protect your files
  • To enable biometric authentication for app access

We do not use your data for advertising, analytics, or share it with third parties.

4. Data Storage

All files are stored locally on your device. We use strong encryption (AES-256) to protect your files. The encryption keys are securely stored in your device's secure storage area.

5. Permissions

VaultX requests the following permissions:

  • Storage Access: To read/write files to your device
  • Camera: To capture photos/videos directly into the app
  • Biometric: To enable fingerprint/face unlock

These permissions are only used for the core functionality of the app and not for any other purposes.

6. Data Deletion

You have complete control over your data:

  • Files can be permanently deleted from the app at any time
  • When you delete the app, all associated data is removed from your device
  • We do not store any backups of your data on remote servers

If you choose to delete original files after importing them to VaultX, the files are permanently removed from their original location but remain stored within the encrypted vault.

7. Data Security

We implement strong security measures to protect your data:

  • AES-256 encryption for all stored files
  • Biometric authentication integration
  • Automatic screen lock when app is minimized
  • No internet connectivity for file transfers (all data stays on your device)

8. Third-Party Services

VaultX does not use any third-party services that access your stored data. All processing is done locally on your device.

9. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@vaultxapp.com
